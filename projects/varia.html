Programming summer camp for children in cooperation with KU Leuven and JCW

In one week 28 children from 12 to 16 years old learn how to program in C++. At the end of the week, everyone was be able to build a simple game (pong) on a Gamebuino. I've organized this camp together with Nick Steen, KU Leuven and JCW. This was the first edition of this camp, and it was sold out in just a few days! After a successful first edition in 2015 of this programming camp a second edition was organized in 2016. Read an article from De Redactie here
