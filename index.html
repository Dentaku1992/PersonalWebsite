Hello, I'm an application and embedded developer from Begijnendijk, Belgium. I hold a Master Degree of Electronics-ICT from the KU Leuven University and a bachelor degree of Aeronautical Sciences from the Katholieke Hogeschool Brugge-Oostende (Vives).

  • Worldline Hackathon

    Organizing a hackathon at Worldline to boost learing and innovation

    A hackathon is a timeboxed, sandboxed programming event where small teams (4-6 people) work on a project with a specific focus to create an initial proof of concept. The Spicathon was a hackathon focused on our new spica platform for Android-based terminals. At Worldline our application developers are already familiar with C programming, while the new Spica platform uses Java and an Android framework. We started by organising a set of training sessions to boost adoption of the new language and the shift towards another paradigm. The “hands-on” spicathon, which focused on creating value-added services (VAS) applications, seemed to us an ideal final step of the training. Benefits include learning from each other and identifying improvements to the platform architecture. This tightly-focused way of working makes it possible to adapt faster to a continually evolving market and to respond to our competition.
